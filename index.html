Chat GPT :-

ChatGPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) is a chatbot launched by OpenAI in November 2022. It is built on top of OpenAI's GPT-3 family of large language models, and is fine-tuned (an approach to transfer learning) with both supervised and reinforcement learning techniques.

Advantages :-

1. Fast and Personalized answers
2. Cost Effective
3. Providing accurate answers for your questions
4. 24/7 Support Available

Disadvantages :-

1. It can End Human Creativity
2. People will stop working hard
3. Children thinking capabilities can be reduced
4. Faith on Wrong Answers
5. Reduce Human Intelligence
